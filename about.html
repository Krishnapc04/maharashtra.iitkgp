About us

We are a group of Maharashtra students who are part of a lively community at our school. Our society was established to promote cultural diversity, inclusivity, and the formation of a strong community for students from Maharashtra. We want to create a student organization at IIT KGP that focuses on preserving and celebrating the culture of Maharashtra. Our goal is to create a supportive environment for Maharashtrian students, promoting unity and acceptance. We aim to provide opportunities for showcasing arts, crafts, and traditions, while also encouraging social responsibility through community projects. Come join our society if you are from Maharashtra or interested in its culture. We warmly welcome you and offer a vibrant community that values diversity, fosters friendships, and creates unforgettable experiences. Let's come together to integrate Maharashtrian culture into our academic institution.
